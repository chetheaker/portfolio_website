about me.

Hey! My name is Che Theaker, welcome to my portfolio and thanks for visiting! I'm a young and aspiring software developer, eager to learn and expand my knowledge about the latest technologies. There's much more to come from myself but in the meantime take a look at my journey so far!

august 2021

education

I attended Monmouth School in South Wales to complete my A level studies. I decided to take Business Studies, Spanish, Mathematics and Further Mathematics in which I was able to achieve 4 'A*'s. I took these subjects because logical and mathematical subjects were the ones which came easiest to me and I also believe that being able to speak multiple languages is an invaluable skill to posses.

january 2021 - present

full stack course

I chose to take this course because of the vast variety of topics and how they lined up with my goals. At the time of writing this, I am just shy of 30% of the way through and I'm throughouly enjoying it. I have so far learnt about HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git and many key web development practices. Throughout this course I'll also learn about more front-end technology such as React.js and back-end technologies such as Node.js and SQL.

february 2021 - present

harvard cs50

To complement my codecademy course learning, I decided to start Harvard's CS50 introduction course to computer science. At the time of writing this I have only completed 2 weeks however, I've still managed to gain a vast knowledge about the fundamentals of compute science and I look forward to expanding this knowledge as I complete this course.
